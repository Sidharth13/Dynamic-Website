Homeopathy

Homeopathy is a medical system based on the belief that the body can cure itself. It is a medical system that was developed in Germany more than 200 years ago...

WHY HOMEOPATHY

Homeopathic medicines are aimed at improving the level of health by stimulating the self-regulatory mechanism.

Homeopathic treatment is an effective method of healing in both acute and chronic conditions, as claimed by millions of patients and thousands of homeopathic doctors. Homeopathy is an effective alternative to antibiotics in infectious diseases, producing no toxic side effects and bringing about rapid recovery. Viral infections are also very well treated with homeopathy.

FACTS AND MYTHS

Myth 1: Homeopathy gives the same white medicines for all diseases

Fact : What seems so is not true. The white pills are only neutral vehicles or carriers of actual medicine that is sprinkled on them. The potent medicine coats the white pills. Different remedies can be prescribed in various potencies to best suit different patients. There are about 3000 medicines and 10 variable potencies (powers of medicines) of each medicine so a minimum of 30,000 various permutation and combinations can be utilized.

Myth 2: Homeopathy is slow to act

Fact : Not really. Homeopathic medicines act rapidly and the effect lasts much longer, as the disease is eradicated from its roots. The time taken for relief depends on the chronicity of the disease.
